Recommendations

Nigerian Treasury Bills (NTB): NTBs are low-risk investments issued by the Central Bank of Nigeria, providing stable returns of 10-15%. Suitable for conservative investors seeking capital preservation with reasonable yields.

Fixed Deposit Account: Fixed Deposit Accounts offer a secure way to invest with expected returns of 7-10%. Ideal for investors prioritizing safety and liquidity over the short to medium term.

Mutual Fund: Mutual funds like STANBIC IBTC Nigerian Equity Fund provide diversification and professional management, targeting returns of 12-18%. Suitable for moderate risk-tolerant investors with long-term goals.

Exchange Traded Fund (ETF): ETFs like NEWGOLD ETF offer diversification and exposure to specific assets, such as gold, with returns of 8-12%. Recommended for portfolio diversification and hedging against market volatility.

Real Estate Investment Trust (REIT): REITs like Skye Shelter Fund provide exposure to real estate without direct ownership, targeting returns of 10-14%. Suitable for investors seeking stable income and potential capital appreciation in real estate.
